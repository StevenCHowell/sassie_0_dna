The initial nucleosome core particle model was generated using psfgen, a plug-in of VMD [1], and based on the PDB-ID 1KX5 X-ray Structure of the nucleosome core particle solved at 1.9 \AA resolution [2].  Using NAMD [3] with the CHARMM27 force fields,[4] we prepared this model for simulations by performing 2000 energy minimization steps followed by 200 MD steps (0.2 ps) then another 2000 energy minimization steps.

[2] C. A. Davey, D. F. Sargent, K. Luger, A. W. Maeder, T. J. Richmond, J. Mol. Biol. 2002, 319, 1097.
